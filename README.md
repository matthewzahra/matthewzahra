## About Me

---
I have just graduated from the Univserty of Oxford where I achieved a First-Class with Distinction in a 4-year Computer Science Integrated Masters. I specialised in formal-verification techniques, both in [novel approaches with reinforcement learning](https://matthewzahra.pythonanywhere.com/project/Robust%20Policy%20Optimisation%20with%20RL) and [algorithmically improving an open-source tool's memory consumption](https://matthewzahra.pythonanywhere.com/project/ProVerif).

After graduating, I am now starting a job as a full time Backend Software engineer in London. My interests outside my work include chess and playing hockey in the London Premier division.
